Food Share

A "Food Share" application is a digital platform designed to connect individuals and communities, enabling them to share surplus food resources, reduce food waste, and combat food insecurity. It facilitates food donations, community meal sharing, and access to food banks, fostering a sense of community and sustainability. Users can donate food, find nearby resources, volunteer, and receive valuable food-related information, all through this user-friendly app.
